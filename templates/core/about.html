About TrainEats

TrainEats is a premier online food ordering and delivery service specifically designed for railway passengers in India. Our mission is to make your train journeys more enjoyable by providing delicious, hygienic, and timely meals right at your seat.

We partner with a wide network of trusted restaurants and vendors at major railway stations across the country to offer a diverse menu of cuisines. Whether you're craving traditional Indian thalis, popular snacks, or continental dishes, TrainEats has something to satisfy every palate.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred food delivery partner for Indian railway passengers, known for our quality, reliability, and customer-centric approach.

Our Commitment

  • Quality & Hygiene: We ensure that all our food partners adhere to strict quality and hygiene standards.
  • Timely Delivery: We understand the importance of time during a train journey. Our delivery network is optimized to ensure your food reaches you on time.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your satisfaction is our top priority. We are always here to assist you with your orders and feedback.
