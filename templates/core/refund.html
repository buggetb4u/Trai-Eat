Refund Policy

Our policy on refunds for orders placed on TrainEats.

1. Eligibility for a Refund

You are eligible for a refund if the food is not delivered, is incorrect, or is of unsatisfactory quality. You must report the issue within 2 hours of the scheduled delivery time.

2. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our customer support with your order details. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.
