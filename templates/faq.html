Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by searching for your train, selecting a vendor, choosing your food items, and proceeding to checkout.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

You can modify your order within 30 minutes of placing it, subject to vendor availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets.

What information is required for delivery?

To ensure a seamless delivery, you must provide your PNR number, a valid mobile number, and the station for food delivery. This information is compulsory for placing an order.

How do I collect my order?

You can collect your order from the vendor's stall at the specified platform when your train arrives.

What if my train is delayed?

We track your train's status and adjust the preparation time accordingly. Your food will be ready when you arrive.

Can I get a refund if I miss my train?

Yes, you can get a full refund if you miss your train. Please contact our support team.

How do I create an account?

Click on the 'Register' button and fill in your details to create an account.

How do I reset my password?

Click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I change my delivery details?

Yes, you can update your delivery details in your profile settings.

How do I become a vendor?

Click on 'Partner With Us' in the footer and fill out the vendor registration form.

What are the requirements to become a vendor?

You need a valid FSSAI license, business registration, and a physical presence at the railway station.

How do vendors get paid?

Vendors receive payments weekly through bank transfer or UPI.
